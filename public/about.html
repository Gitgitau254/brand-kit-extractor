▦ Brand Kit Extractor

About

Brand Kit Extractor helps designers and developers quickly understand how a website is styled.

Paste any domain and extract its color palette, typography, spacing, and UI tokens — structured and ready to reuse. Exports are available as CSS variables, JSON, and Tailwind config.

It’s fast, privacy-friendly, and requires no accounts — built to save time, reduce guesswork, and make design systems easier to work with.
